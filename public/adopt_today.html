Adopt a Cat

We estimate our adoption fees based on the cost of rescuing and housing our cats. Chances are that we have spent far more on your new friend than the adoption fee costs. This is why we appreciate extra donations to help other cats as they wait to be adopted. This could include something from our wishlist, or becoming a sponsor.

Paper applications are available at our location. Once this has been completed, it can take up to 1-3 days to process your application. Once we have processed your application, we will notifiy you right away.

We do not adopt to anyone under 21

What our fees include

  • Microchipping
  • Free registration
  • FELV/FIV/Heartworm testing
  • Parasite/worms testing
  • Spay/Neuter
